Welcome to GCSE Revision

Your all-in-one revision toolkit for English Literature, Maths, and the Sciences. Flashcards, quizzes, spaced repetition, and more.

🃏

Flashcards & Spaced Repetition

Tap a card to reveal the analysis. Rate it Hard, OK, or Easy — the app schedules your next review automatically using the SM-2 algorithm.

🧪

Quizzes & Exam Mode

Use Test Mode for multiple-choice practice. Exam Mode is timed — perfect for simulating the real thing. The adaptive mode focuses on your weakest cards.

📝

Notes & Essay Planner

Search your Science and Maths notes. For English Lit, the Essay tab builds a structured essay plan from your quotes — pick a theme and it scaffolds the paragraphs.

🎯

You're all set!

Your progress saves automatically. Use the Dashboard tab to track theme mastery. Good luck — you've got this! 💪
